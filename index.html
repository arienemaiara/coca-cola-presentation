Coca-Cola Rebranding

Design Thinking Course - CSUN

Problem

People are drinking less Coke because they're trying to have a healthier life.

New Positioning

Make our target audience see that they deserve to have a Coke no matter what kind of life they have.

We’re not focusing on the fact that Coca-Cola isn’t health or there is a lot of sugar on it, but the fact that the person deserves all the happiness of having a Coke despite of all those things.

You deserve this.

Você merece isso.

Te lo mereces.

Vous le mérite.
